Micro-interactions are those subtle design moments that fulfill a single function: delighting the user while they perform a task.

From the soft "pop" of a button when pressed to the progress bar that seems to advance with elegance, these details build a relationship of trust with the user. They are not ornaments; they are the emotional glue of the interface.
